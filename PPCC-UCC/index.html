About The Choir

PPCC Choir is a joint choir of the various denominations which seek to minister unto people through hymns, choral music and song. Some activities of the PPCC choir include spearheading of carols service, having dawn ministrations, socialization Video recordings/Concerts of which seek to strengthen the spiritual and social lives of members and the University community.
